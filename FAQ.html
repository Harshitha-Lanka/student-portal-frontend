Frequently Asked Questions – Andhra University

Admissions

Applications for UG courses can be submitted online via the Andhra University Admissions Portal. Specific entrance tests like AUEET may be required.

Deadlines vary each year. Refer to the official AU website’s admission notifications section for updated dates.

Academics

The academic year generally begins in July or August depending on the program.

Visit the Academics section on the AU website to download syllabi and semester schedules.

Hostels & Facilities

Hostel applications are available at the Dean of Student Welfare’s office and must be submitted before the academic session starts.

Yes, Andhra University provides separate hostels for boys and girls with security and facilities like mess, Wi-Fi, and reading rooms.

Fees & Online Payments

Yes, fee payment can be made through the official AU payment portal using debit/credit cards or net banking.

Receipts are generated immediately after payment on the portal and can be downloaded from your student dashboard.
